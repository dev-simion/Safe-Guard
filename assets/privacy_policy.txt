Privacy Policy for SafeZone App

Effective Date: October 18, 2025

Thank you for using SafeZone, a safety alert and reporting application
developed to help students and community members quickly connect with
local emergency services during distress or unsafe situations. Your
privacy and security are extremely important to us.

1. Information We Collect

-   Personal Information: When you sign up, we may collect your name,
    phone number, school name, and emergency contact details.
-   Location Data: SafeZone collects location information when you
    activate an SOS alert or when location sharing is enabled to provide
    accurate assistance.
-   Media (Photos, Videos, Audio): Users can upload photos or videos as
    evidence of incidents. These files are securely uploaded and
    encrypted.
-   Device Information: We may collect device identifiers, operating
    system version, and app usage data for performance improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

-   To alert emergency services and designated contacts in real-time
    during distress situations.
-   To improve app performance, reliability, and safety.
-   To generate anonymized data reports that help schools and
    authorities understand and reduce bullying or unsafe activities.
-   To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.

3. How We Share Your Information

-   With emergency responders, school administrators, or trusted
    contacts when an SOS alert is triggered.
-   With law enforcement authorities if legally required or for safety
    purposes.
-   We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and secure cloud storage to protect your personal
data. Only authorized personnel and verified responders can access
sensitive information.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for safety, reporting, or legal
compliance. You may request deletion of your account or data at any
time.

6. User Controls and Rights

You can: - Request deletion of your data. - Update or correct your
personal information. - Revoke permissions such as location or camera
access.

7. Permissions Used

SafeZone requires the following permissions for functionality: -
Internet access: For sending alerts and uploading data. - Location
access: To pinpoint your location during emergencies. - Camera &
Storage: For photo/video uploads as evidence. - Display over apps: For
showing floating SOS button or alerts.
